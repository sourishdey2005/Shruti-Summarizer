Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 25, 2024

Welcome to Shruti Summaries. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

1. Information We Handle

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. The application processes the news article text you provide solely for the purpose of generating a summary.

  • Article Text: The text you paste into the application is sent to the Google Gemini API for processing. We do not log or store this text on our servers. It is handled in-memory and discarded after the summary is generated.
  • API Key: This application uses an API key configured in its environment to communicate with the Google Gemini API. Your personal API key is not required or collected.

2. How Information is Used

The article text you provide is used exclusively to generate a summary. The text is sent to the Google Gemini API, which processes it and returns a summary. The summary is then displayed in the app and used for text-to-speech playback on your device.

3. Data Storage

This application is stateless. It does not have a database and does not store any of your data. All processing happens on-demand, and the information is not retained after you close the application session.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the Google Gemini API to process content. Your interaction with this third-party service is governed by Google's Privacy Policy. We are not responsible for their data handling practices.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by an updated "Last Updated" date.
